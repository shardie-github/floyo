## Channel Prioritization

### Phase 1 (Months 1-2): Foundation
1. **Product Hunt Launch** - One-time high-impact event
2. **Hacker News** - Technical audience, high conversion
3. **SEO Landing Pages** - Long-term organic growth
4. **Referral Program** - Viral growth foundation

**Goal:** 1,000 signups

### Phase 2 (Months 3-4): Growth Experiments
1. **GitHub Marketplace** - Platform integration
2. **Twitter/X** - Social media presence
3. **Content Marketing** - SEO and authority building
4. **Developer Communities** - Community engagement

**Goal:** 5,000 signups

### Phase 3 (Months 5-6): Scale
1. **Paid Advertising** (if profitable) - Scale proven channels
2. **Partnerships** - Integration partnerships
3. **Enterprise Sales** - High-value customers

**Goal:** 10,000 signups

## Channel-Specific Strategies

### Product Hunt
- **Pre-Launch:** Build email list, engage community
- **Launch Day:** Monitor metrics, engage comments
- **Post-Launch:** Follow up with blog post, track long-tail traffic

### Hacker News
- **Post Timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM PT
- **Engagement:** Respond to comments authentically
- **Follow-Up:** Post updates, technical deep-dives

### SEO Landing Pages
- **Keyword Research:** Target long-tail keywords
- **Content:** High-quality, actionable content
- **Optimization:** On-page SEO, structured data, internal linking

### Referral Program
- **Incentives:** Free month, discount, or feature unlock
- **Sharing:** One-click invite, shareable links
- **Tracking:** UTM parameters, referral attribution

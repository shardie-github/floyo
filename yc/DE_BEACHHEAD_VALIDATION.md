## Overview

This document validates Floyo's beachhead market: **Solo E-commerce Operators** (1-2 person teams running e-commerce businesses). Based on Disciplined Entrepreneurship framework, we need to validate: problem urgency, willingness to pay, feature priorities, and buying process.

---

## Beachhead Market Definition

### Target Customer
**Solo E-commerce Operator**
- **Role:** Solo operator or 1-2 person team running e-commerce business
- **Revenue:** $10k-$50k/month in sales
- **Age:** 28-40
- **Location:** North America, Europe, Australia
- **Tech Stack:** Shopify + TikTok Ads + Meta Ads + Zapier + Google Sheets + various tools
- **Team Size:** 1-2 people
- **Tool Count:** 8-15 different SaaS tools

### Why This Beachhead
1. **High Pain:** 2-3 hours/day on manual data sync = $1,500-$3,000/month value
2. **Clear ROI:** Time saved is measurable and valuable
3. **Willingness to Pay:** Budget exists ($300-500/month for tools)
4. **Viral Potential:** Share tools in communities (Shopify forums, TikTok creator groups)
5. **Defensible Entry Point:** Deep integration with specific tools (Shopify, TikTok Ads, Meta Ads, Zapier)

---

## Validation Interview Plan

### Interview Goals
1. Validate problem exists and is urgent
2. Understand current workarounds
3. Test willingness to pay
4. Identify feature priorities
5. Understand buying process

### Interview Questions

#### Problem Validation
1. **"Tell me about your typical day managing your e-commerce business."**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "How much time do you spend manually syncing data between tools?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What's the most frustrating part of your workflow?"

2. **"How many tools do you use daily? Which ones?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "Do you know which tools you actually use vs. just subscribe to?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "Have you ever paid for a tool you don't use?"

3. **"Tell me about a time when a broken automation caused problems."**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "How often do automations break?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "How do you discover when something breaks?"

#### Current Workarounds
4. **"What do you currently do to manage your tool stack?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "Do you have a system for tracking which tools you use?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "How do you discover new integrations?"

5. **"What tools or processes have you tried to solve this problem?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "Why didn't they work?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What's missing?"

#### Willingness to Pay
6. **"How much do you currently spend on tools per month?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "Would you pay $29/month for a tool that saves you 2 hours/day?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What's the maximum you'd pay?"

7. **"What would make you switch from your current solution?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What features are must-haves?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What would make you cancel?"

#### Feature Priorities
8. **"If you could have one feature that solves your biggest pain, what would it be?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "Why is that most important?"
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What would you do with the time saved?"

9. **"What features would you expect in a tool like Floyo?"**
   - [ ] Follow-up: "What's nice-to-have vs. must-have?"

#### Buying Process
10. **"How do you typically discover new tools?"**
    - [ ] Follow-up: "What makes you try a new tool?"
    - [ ] Follow-up: "Who else is involved in the decision?"

11. **"What's your process for evaluating a new tool?"**
    - [ ] Follow-up: "How long does it take to decide?"
    - [ ] Follow-up: "What's the biggest barrier to trying something new?"

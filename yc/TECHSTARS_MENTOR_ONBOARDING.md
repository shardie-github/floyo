### Product Overview
Floyo automatically tracks how developers and teams use files and tools, then uses AI to suggest integrations and automations that eliminate manual work and optimize workflows.

**Target Users:**
1. Solo E-commerce Operators (primary)
2. Solo Full-Stack Developers (primary)

**Key Value Props:**
- Privacy-first workflow tracking
- Automatic integration discovery
- Concrete, actionable suggestions
- Zero configuration required

### 2. Technical Mentorship

## Planned Experiments

### Experiment #1: Invite CTA Copy Test
**Planned Start:** [TODO]  
**Status:** Planned

**Hypothesis:**
Changing invite CTA copy from "Invite Friends" to "Get 1 Month Free" will increase invite click rate by 20%.

**Test Method:**
A/B test with 50/50 split, measure click rate on invite button.

**Success Criteria:**
- Click rate increase > 20%
- Statistical significance (p < 0.05)

**Dependencies:**
- A/B testing framework
- Frontend invite page

### Experiment #2: Onboarding Flow Length Test
**Planned Start:** [TODO]  
**Status:** Planned

**Hypothesis:**
Reducing onboarding from 5 steps to 3 steps will increase completion rate by 15% without reducing activation rate.

**Test Method:**
A/B test with 50/50 split, measure completion rate and activation rate.

**Success Criteria:**
- Completion rate increase > 15%
- Activation rate maintained or improved
- Statistical significance (p < 0.05)

**Dependencies:**
- Onboarding flow refactor
- Activation tracking

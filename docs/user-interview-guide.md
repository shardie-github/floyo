# User Interview Guide
## Floyo - File Usage Pattern Tracking & Integration Suggestions

**Purpose:** Collect feedback from beta users to validate product-market fit and identify improvement opportunities.

**Interview Duration:** 30-45 minutes  
**Target:** 5-10 interviews per sprint

---

## Pre-Interview Setup

### Before the Interview
- [ ] Review user's profile (ICP, usage patterns if available)
- [ ] Prepare demo environment (test account, sample data)
- [ ] Set up recording (with permission)
- [ ] Prepare note-taking template

### Interview Structure
1. **Introduction** (5 min)
2. **Background & Context** (10 min)
3. **Product Demo & Usage** (15 min)
4. **Feedback & Questions** (10 min)
5. **Closing** (5 min)

---

## Interview Script

### 1. Introduction (5 minutes)

**Script:**
"Hi [Name], thanks for taking the time to talk with me today. I'm [Your Name], and I'm working on Floyo, a tool that helps developers track their file usage patterns and discover useful integrations.

Today, I'd like to understand your experience using Floyo and get your feedback on what's working and what could be improved. This will take about 30-45 minutes.

Is it okay if I record this conversation? It helps me focus on listening rather than taking notes. The recording will only be used internally and won't be shared publicly."

---

### 2. Background & Context (10 minutes)

**Questions:**

1. **Tell me about yourself and your work.**
   - What's your role?
   - What kind of projects do you work on?
   - How long have you been in this role?

2. **What tools do you use in your daily work?**
   - IDEs/editors?
   - Project management tools?
   - Other productivity tools?

3. **What challenges do you face with your current workflow?**
   - Context switching?
   - Tool discovery?
   - Workflow optimization?
   - Privacy concerns?

4. **How did you hear about Floyo?**
   - What made you sign up?
   - What problem were you hoping it would solve?

---

### 3. Product Demo & Usage (15 minutes)

**Script:**
"Now I'd like to walk through Floyo with you. I'll show you the main features, and I'd love to hear your thoughts as we go."

**Demo Flow:**
1. **Onboarding Experience**
   - "Can you walk me through your onboarding experience?"
   - "What was confusing or unclear?"
   - "What did you like?"

2. **Dashboard**
   - "Let's look at your dashboard. What do you see here?"
   - "Is this useful? Why or why not?"
   - "What's missing?"

3. **Insights & Recommendations**
   - "Have you seen any insights or recommendations?"
   - "Were they relevant? Why or why not?"
   - "Did you act on any of them?"

4. **File Tracking**
   - "Have you installed the file tracking client?"
   - "If yes: How has it been working?"
   - "If no: What stopped you?"

5. **Privacy Controls**
   - "Have you looked at the privacy settings?"
   - "Do you feel comfortable with what's being tracked?"
   - "What concerns do you have?"

---

### 4. Feedback & Questions (10 minutes)

**Questions:**

1. **Overall Experience**
   - "On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate your experience with Floyo?"
   - "What would make it a 10?"

2. **Value Proposition**
   - "What value have you gotten from Floyo so far?"
   - "What value are you hoping to get?"

3. **Pain Points**
   - "What's been frustrating or confusing?"
   - "What features are missing?"
   - "What would make you use Floyo more?"

4. **Recommendations**
   - "Would you recommend Floyo to a colleague? Why or why not?"
   - "What would you tell them about it?"

5. **Pricing**
   - "What would you be willing to pay for Floyo?"
   - "What features would justify that price?"

---

### 5. Closing (5 minutes)

**Script:**
"Thank you so much for your time and feedback. This is really helpful.

Is there anything else you'd like to share or ask about?

We'll be making updates based on feedback like yours. Would you be open to follow-up interviews as we add new features?

Thanks again!"

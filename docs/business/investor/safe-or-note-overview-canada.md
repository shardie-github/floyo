## SAFE Overview

### What is a SAFE?

**SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)** is a convertible security that converts to equity upon a future priced round or liquidity event.

**Key Features:**
- **Conversion:** Converts to equity at discount (typically 15-25%)
- **Valuation Cap:** Maximum valuation at conversion (if applicable)
- **No Interest:** No interest payments (unlike convertible notes)
- **No Maturity:** No maturity date (converts or exits)

### SAFE Terms (Typical)

**Discount:** 15-25% (typical)  
**Valuation Cap:** $500K-2M CAD (typical for seed)  
**Conversion Trigger:** Next priced round ($250K+)

### Advantages

- **Simple:** Fewer terms than convertible notes
- **Fast:** Quick to execute (standard documents)
- **Founder-Friendly:** No interest, no maturity

### Disadvantages

- **Dilution:** Dilution at conversion (founder equity)
- **Valuation Uncertainty:** Valuation determined at conversion

## Convertible Note Overview

### What is a Convertible Note?

**Convertible Note** is a debt instrument that converts to equity upon a future priced round.

**Key Features:**
- **Conversion:** Converts to equity at discount (typically 15-25%)
- **Interest Rate:** 2-8% annual interest (typical)
- **Maturity Date:** 12-24 months (typical)
- **Conversion Trigger:** Next priced round ($250K+)

### Convertible Note Terms (Typical)

**Discount:** 15-25% (typical)  
**Interest Rate:** 2-8% annual  
**Maturity Date:** 12-24 months  
**Conversion Trigger:** Next priced round ($250K+)

### Advantages

- **Familiar:** Well-understood structure
- **Interest:** Interest payments (investor benefit)

### Disadvantages

- **Interest:** Interest payments (founder cost)
- **Maturity:** Maturity date pressure (if not converted)

## Canada-Specific Considerations

### Tax Implications

**SAFE:**
- **Tax Treatment:** Generally treated as equity (not debt)
- **Founder Tax:** No immediate tax (converts at future round)
- **Investor Tax:** Capital gains treatment (if qualified)

**Convertible Note:**
- **Tax Treatment:** Debt instrument (interest taxable)
- **Founder Tax:** Interest deductions (if applicable)
- **Investor Tax:** Interest income (taxable)

### Legal Considerations

- **Securities Law:** Comply with Canadian securities laws
- **Investor Qualifications:** Accredited investor requirements (if applicable)
- **Legal Counsel:** Consult Canadian legal counsel

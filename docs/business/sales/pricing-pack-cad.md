## Pricing Overview

floyo offers three pricing tiers designed for solo operators, freelancers, and small teams.

**Currency:** CAD (Canadian Dollar)  
**Tax:** GST/HST 13% (Ontario) applied at checkout  
**Billing:** Monthly or annual (save 17% with annual)

---

## Pricing Tiers

### Free Tier
**Price:** $0 CAD/month (forever)

**Features:**
- File system monitoring (up to 10 directories)
- Basic pattern detection
- Community support (forum, Discord)
- Limited workflows (5 workflows)

**Ideal For:**
- Trying floyo
- Basic automation needs
- Privacy-conscious users testing the product

**Limitations:**
- 10 directories max
- 5 workflows max
- No email support
- Basic pattern detection only

---

### Starter Tier
**Price:** $12 CAD/month or $120 CAD/year (save 17%)

**Features:**
- Everything in Free
- Unlimited directories
- Advanced pattern detection
- Integration suggestions (sample code)
- Email support (48h SLA)
- Priority feature requests

**Ideal For:**
- Solo operators
- Freelancers
- Small teams (1-2 people)

**ROI:**
- Break-even at 1 hour saved/week (assuming $30 CAD/hour opportunity cost)
- Save 5-10 hours/week → $150-300 CAD/week value

---

### Pro Tier

**Features:**
- Everything in Starter

- Workflow versioning and rollback

**Ideal For:**
- Power users

**ROI:**

## Pricing FAQs

### Q: Can I switch plans anytime?
A: Yes. Upgrade/downgrade anytime in Settings → Account → Change Plan.

### Q: What happens if I cancel?
A: Cancellation takes effect at end of billing period. Paid features available until then.

### Q: Do you offer refunds?
A: Yes. 30-day money-back guarantee (see Refund Policy).

### Q: Is there a free trial?
A: Free tier available forever (no credit card required).

### Q: Can I get a discount?
A: Annual subscriptions save 17%. No other discounts currently.

## Overview

This policy outlines floyo's refund and cancellation procedures for subscription services.

**Refund Period:** 30 days from purchase  
**Cancellation:** Anytime, takes effect at end of billing period  
**Processing Time:** 14 business days

---

## 1. Refund Policy

### 1.1 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

**Eligibility:**
- All new subscriptions (Starter, Pro tiers)
- First-time customers only
- Within 30 days of initial purchase

**Refund Process:**
1. Request refund: Email [YOUR-EMAIL] with subject "Refund Request"
2. Provide information: Email address, subscription tier, purchase date
3. Review: Processed within 5 business days
4. Refund: Processed within 14 business days (original payment method)

**Refund Amount:**
- Full refund (pro-rated if annual subscription)
- Processing fees (if applicable) deducted

### 1.2 Non-Refundable
- **Free Tier:** N/A (no payment)
- **Renewals:** Subscription renewals (after initial 30-day period)
- **Partial Refunds:** Not available (all-or-nothing)

---

## 2. Cancellation Policy

### 2.1 Cancellation Process

**Self-Service:**
1. Log in to floyo
2. Go to Settings → Account → Cancel Subscription
3. Confirm cancellation
4. Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period

**Email Cancellation:**
1. Email [YOUR-EMAIL] with subject "Cancel Subscription"
2. Provide email address (for account verification)
3. Confirmation email sent within 48 hours

### 2.2 Cancellation Timing
- **Immediate:** Cancellation requested immediately
- **Effective Date:** End of current billing period (monthly or annual)
- **Access:** Paid features available until end of billing period

### 2.3 No Cancellation Fees
- No fees for cancellation
- No penalties for early cancellation
- Prorated refunds not available (after 30-day period)

## 3. Subscription Changes

### 3.1 Upgrade/Downgrade
- **Upgrade:** Immediate (pro-rated billing)
- **Downgrade:** Takes effect at end of current billing period
- **Changes:** Settings → Account → Change Plan

### 3.2 Billing Cycle Changes
- **Monthly to Annual:** Pro-rated refund, annual billing starts immediately
- **Annual to Monthly:** No refund, monthly billing starts at end of annual period

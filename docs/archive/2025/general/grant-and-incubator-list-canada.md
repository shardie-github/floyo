## üí∞ Government Grants

### **1. IRAP (Industrial Research Assistance Program)**
- **Organization**: National Research Council Canada (NRC)
- **Program**: IRAP
- **Amount**: Up to $50,000 CAD (Phase 1), up to $500,000 CAD (Phase 2)
- **Eligibility**: Canadian SMEs, technology innovation
- **Deadline**: Rolling
- **Website**: https://nrc.canada.ca/en/irap
- **Notes**: Focus on R&D, technical innovation

---

### **2. SR&ED (Scientific Research and Experimental Development)**
- **Organization**: Canada Revenue Agency (CRA)
- **Program**: SR&ED Tax Credit
- **Amount**: Up to 35% of eligible R&D expenses (refundable for CCPC)
- **Eligibility**: Canadian corporations conducting R&D
- **Deadline**: 18 months after tax year-end
- **Website**: https://www.canada.ca/en/revenue-agency/services/scientific-research-experimental-development-tax-incentive-program.html
- **Notes**: Tax credit, not direct grant

---

### **3. CanExport SMEs**
- **Organization**: Trade Commissioner Service
- **Program**: CanExport SMEs
- **Amount**: Up to $50,000 CAD
- **Eligibility**: Canadian SMEs, export activities
- **Deadline**: Rolling
- **Website**: https://www.tradecommissioner.gc.ca/canexport-sme/index.aspx
- **Notes**: Focus on export, international expansion

---

### **4. Digital Adoption Program (DAP)**
- **Organization**: Various provinces
- **Program**: DAP
- **Amount**: Varies by province
- **Eligibility**: SMEs adopting digital technology
- **Deadline**: Varies
- **Website**: Check provincial websites
- **Notes**: Province-specific programs

---

### **5. Futurpreneur Canada**
- **Organization**: Futurpreneur Canada
- **Program**: Startup Loan Program
- **Amount**: Up to $60,000 CAD (loan + mentorship)
- **Eligibility**: Canadian entrepreneurs aged 18-39
- **Deadline**: Rolling
- **Website**: https://www.futurpreneur.ca
- **Notes**: Loan program, mentorship included

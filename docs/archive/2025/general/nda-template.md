### **1. PURPOSE**

The parties wish to explore potential business relationship and may disclose confidential information to each other in connection therewith.

---

### **2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION**

"Confidential Information" means all non-public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by Disclosing Party to Receiving Party, including but not limited to:

- Business plans, financial information, revenue data
- Customer lists, user data, market research
- Technical specifications, algorithms, source code
- Product roadmaps, feature plans
- Marketing strategies, pricing information
- Any information marked "Confidential" or "Proprietary"

**Exclusions:**
Confidential Information does not include information that:
- Is publicly available or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement
- Was known to Receiving Party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by Receiving Party without use of Confidential Information
- Is rightfully received from third party without confidentiality obligation

---

### **3. OBLIGATIONS**

Receiving Party agrees to:
- Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence
- Use Confidential Information solely for purpose of exploring business relationship
- Not disclose Confidential Information to third parties without prior written consent
- Take reasonable precautions to protect Confidential Information
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon request or termination of Agreement

---

### **4. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES**

Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information to:
- Employees, contractors, or advisors who need to know for business relationship purposes
- Recipients must be bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as this Agreement

---

### **5. TERM**

This Agreement shall remain in effect for [NUMBER] years from Effective Date, or until terminated by either party with [NUMBER] days written notice.

**Survival:** Confidentiality obligations shall survive termination of Agreement.

---

### **6. REMEDIES**

Receiving Party acknowledges that breach of this Agreement would cause irreparable harm to Disclosing Party. Disclosing Party shall be entitled to:
- Injunctive relief without bond
- Actual damages
- Reasonable attorneys' fees and costs

---

### **7. RETURN OF MATERIALS**

Upon request or termination, Receiving Party shall:
- Return all documents and materials containing Confidential Information
- Delete all electronic copies
- Certify in writing that all Confidential Information has been returned or destroyed

---

### **8. NO LICENSE**

This Agreement does not grant any license or right to use Confidential Information except as expressly stated herein.

---

### **9. GOVERNING LAW**

This Agreement shall be governed by laws of [Province/Territory], Canada.

---

### **10. GENERAL PROVISIONS**

**Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes entire agreement regarding confidentiality.

**Modifications:** Modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.

**Severability:** If any provision is invalid, remaining provisions shall remain in effect.

## üéØ Pre-Fundraising Checklist

### **6 Months Before**

- [ ] **Financials**: Prepare 12-month financial projections
- [ ] **Metrics**: Establish baseline metrics (DAU, MRR, CAC, LTV)
- [ ] **Team**: Identify key hires needed post-funding
- [ ] **Market Research**: Validate TAM/SAM/SOM
- [ ] **Competitive Analysis**: Document competitive landscape
- [ ] **Legal**: Incorporate (if not already), prepare cap table

### **3 Months Before**

- [ ] **Pitch Deck**: Create initial version (see template below)
- [ ] **Financial Model**: Build detailed 3-5 year model
- [ ] **Data Room**: Prepare investor data room (see below)
- [ ] **Advisors**: Recruit 2-3 advisors
- [ ] **Warm Introductions**: Start reaching out to investors
- [ ] **Due Diligence Prep**: Organize all legal, financial, technical docs

### **1 Month Before**

- [ ] **Pitch Deck**: Finalize version 10+
- [ ] **Demo**: Prepare live demo (backup: video demo)
- [ ] **One-Pager**: Create 1-page executive summary
- [ ] **Investor List**: Finalize target list (50-100 investors)
- [ ] **Schedule Meetings**: Book first 10 meetings
- [ ] **Legal**: Engage lawyer for SAFE/Note preparation

## üìä Pitch Deck Template (12 Slides)

- Tagline: "Local-first workflow automation for solo operators"

- Contact info

- Solo operators waste 10+ hours/week on repetitive file tasks
- Existing solutions: Cloud-dependent, expensive, complex
- Market size: [TAM/SAM/SOM]

### **Slide 3: Solution**
- floyo: Local-first workflow automation tool
- Key features: Pattern detection, API integrations, privacy-first
- Demo screenshot/video

### **Slide 4: Market Opportunity**
- TAM: $X billion CAD
- SAM: $X million CAD
- SOM: $X million CAD (3-year target)
- Market trends: Remote work, privacy concerns, automation growth

### **Slide 5: Product**
- Core features
- User workflow
- Screenshots/demo
- Technology stack

### **Slide 6: Traction**
- Users: [number]
- MRR: $[amount] CAD
- Growth rate: [X]% MoM
- Key metrics: DAU, retention, churn
- Customer testimonials

### **Slide 7: Business Model**
- Pricing tiers: Free, Pro ($12 CAD/month), Enterprise ($49 CAD/month)
- Revenue streams: Subscriptions, enterprise licenses
- Unit economics: CAC, LTV, LTV:CAC ratio
- Projected revenue: [3-year forecast]

### **Slide 8: Go-to-Market**
- Channels: Product Hunt, Reddit, LinkedIn, partnerships
- Customer acquisition: Content marketing, community, referrals
- Growth strategy: [outline]

### **Slide 9: Competition**
- Competitive landscape (2x2 matrix)
- floyo's differentiation: Local-first, privacy, affordability
- Competitive advantages: [list]

### **Slide 10: Team**
- Founder: [bio, relevant experience]
- Advisors: [names, backgrounds]
- Key hires planned: [roles]

### **Slide 11: Financials**
- Current: Revenue, burn rate, runway
- Projections: 3-year revenue forecast
- Use of funds: [breakdown]
- Milestones: [key milestones for 12/24 months]

### **Slide 12: Ask**
- Funding amount: $[amount] CAD
- Valuation: Pre-money $[amount] CAD
- Use of funds: [breakdown]
- Timeline: [close date]

## üìã Due Diligence Prep

### **Common Questions**

**Financial:**
- What's your burn rate?
- What's your runway?
- What are your unit economics?
- What's your revenue model?

**Product:**
- What's your tech stack?
- How do you handle security/privacy?
- What's your product roadmap?
- What are your key differentiators?

**Market:**
- What's your TAM/SAM/SOM?
- Who are your competitors?
- What's your go-to-market strategy?
- What are your growth metrics?

**Team:**
- What's your background?
- Who's on your team?
- What key hires do you need?
- Who are your advisors?

**Legal:**
- Are you incorporated?
- Who owns the IP?
- Are there any outstanding legal issues?
- What's your cap table?

## ‚úÖ Meeting Prep Checklist

- [ ] Review investor's portfolio (companies, stages)
- [ ] Prepare answers to common questions
- [ ] Test demo (backup: video demo)
- [ ] Print pitch deck (backup: digital)
- [ ] Prepare one-pager
- [ ] Research investor's background

**During Meeting:**
- [ ] Start with problem/solution (hook)
- [ ] Show demo early (if applicable)
- [ ] Focus on traction/metrics
- [ ] Address competition head-on
- [ ] Be honest about challenges
- [ ] Ask for feedback

**After Meeting:**
- [ ] Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- [ ] Include pitch deck/data room link
- [ ] Answer any follow-up questions
- [ ] Follow up in 1 week if no response

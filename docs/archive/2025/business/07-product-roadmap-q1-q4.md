# Product Roadmap — Q1-Q4 2024

## Overview

**Vision:** floyo becomes the default local-first workflow automation tool for privacy-conscious solo operators and small teams in Canada.

**Strategy:** MVP launch → Growth → Scale → Enterprise

---

## Q1 2024: MVP Launch

### Goals
- Launch MVP with core features
- Achieve 100 active users (WAU)
- 40% activation rate
- 25% 7-day retention

### Features

#### Core (Must Have)
- ✅ File system monitoring (configurable directories)
- ✅ Pattern detection engine (temporal, relational, dependency)
- ✅ Integration suggestions (sample code provided)
- ✅ Workflow execution (local, no cloud)
- ✅ Basic UI (desktop app, Electron)
- ✅ Privacy controls (consent-gated telemetry)

#### Launch Readiness
- ✅ Privacy policy (PIPEDA-compliant)
- ✅ Terms of Service
- ✅ App Store listings (Apple, Google Play)
- ✅ Support documentation (FAQ, tutorials)
- ✅ Beta testing (20 users, 2 weeks)

### Metrics
- **WAU:** 100+
- **Activation Rate:** ≥ 40%
- **7-Day Retention:** ≥ 25%
- **Support Tickets:** < 5% of users

### Milestones
- **Week 1-2:** Beta testing, bug fixes
- **Week 3-4:** App Store submissions, marketing prep
- **Week 5-6:** Public launch (Product Hunt, Reddit, Indie Hackers)
- **Week 7-8:** Post-launch optimization (based on feedback)

## Feature Prioritization Framework

### Priority Levels
- **P0 (Critical):** Blocking MVP launch (file monitoring, pattern detection)
- **P1 (High):** Core value proposition (integration suggestions, workflow execution)
- **P2 (Medium):** Growth features (cloud backup, team features)
- **P3 (Low):** Nice-to-have (advanced analytics, marketplace)

### Evaluation Criteria
1. **User Value:** How many users benefit?
2. **Business Value:** Revenue impact, retention impact
3. **Technical Complexity:** Development effort, risk
4. **Market Demand:** User requests, competitive pressure

---

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Activation Rate
- **Mitigation:** Improve onboarding, better pattern detection accuracy
- **Timeline:** Q1 (post-launch optimization)

### Risk 2: Low Retention
- **Mitigation:** Community features, workflow templates, email notifications
- **Timeline:** Q2 (growth features)

### Risk 3: Slow Growth
- **Mitigation:** Marketing push, partnerships, influencer outreach
- **Timeline:** Q2-Q3 (growth phase)

### Risk 4: Enterprise Sales Cycle
- **Mitigation:** Start early (Q3), build relationships, case studies
- **Timeline:** Q3-Q4 (enterprise prep)

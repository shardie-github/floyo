## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide our services:

- **Account Information:** Email address (for account creation and support)
- **Billing Information:** Payment information processed by Stripe (we do not store payment details)
- **Usage Data:** Optional telemetry data (opt-in only, see Section 2.2)
- **Support Information:** Information you provide when contacting support

### 1.2 File System Data (Local Processing)
**Important:** floyo processes file system data locally on your device. We do not:
- Upload your files to our servers
- Store your files in the cloud
- Access your files without your explicit permission

File system monitoring occurs only on directories you configure. All processing is local and encrypted.

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
If you opt-in to telemetry (see Section 2.2), we may collect:
- Usage analytics (feature usage, workflow execution)
- Error logs (crash reports, error messages)
- Performance metrics (app performance, system resources)

**Note:** Telemetry is opt-in only. You can disable it at any time in settings.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local-First Architecture
**Core Principle:** floyo uses a local-first architecture. Your file system data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to our servers.

### 4.2 Encryption
- **Local Storage:** File system data is encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- **Transmission:** If telemetry is enabled, data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.3)

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Account Information:** Retained while your account is active
- **File System Data:** Stored locally on your device (you control retention)
- **Telemetry Data:** Retained for 12 months (if opt-in), then anonymized or deleted

### 4.4 Data Deletion
You can request deletion of your personal information at any time:
- **Account Deletion:** Delete your account in Settings → Account → Delete Account
- **Data Export:** Export your data in Settings → Privacy → Export Data
- **Contact:** Email [YOUR-EMAIL] to request deletion

## 11. PIPEDA Compliance Statement

floyo complies with PIPEDA's 10 fair information principles:

1. **Accountability:** We are responsible for protecting your personal information.
2. **Identifying Purposes:** We collect information only for specified purposes.
3. **Consent:** We obtain consent before collecting personal information (opt-in for telemetry).
4. **Limiting Collection:** We collect only necessary information.
5. **Limiting Use, Disclosure, and Retention:** We use information only for stated purposes and retain it only as long as necessary.
6. **Accuracy:** We maintain accurate information (you can update it).
7. **Safeguards:** We protect information with appropriate security measures.
8. **Openness:** This Privacy Policy discloses our practices.
9. **Individual Access:** You can access your personal information (Section 5.1).
10. **Challenging Compliance:** You can challenge our compliance (contact [YOUR-EMAIL]).

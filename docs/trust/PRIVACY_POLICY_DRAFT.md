# Privacy Policy Draft

**Last Updated:** 2025-01-XX  
**Version:** 1.0 (Draft)

*This is a draft privacy policy aligned to GDPR and PIPEDA. Legal review recommended before publication.*

## Plain Language Summary

We collect minimal data to provide our service. You control what data we collect and how we use it. You can export or delete your data at any time. We never sell your data.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- Email address
- Username (optional)
- Password (hashed)
- Profile information (optional)

### 1.2 Usage Data (Opt-in)
- Events you choose to track
- Timestamps
- Feature usage (anonymized)

### 1.3 Technical Data
- IP address (for security)
- Browser type and version
- Device information

### 1.4 What We Don't Collect
- Passwords (stored as hashes only)
- Message contents (unless explicitly enabled)
- Keystrokes or screenshots
- Payment card details (processed by third-party)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Lawful Bases (GDPR)

We process your data under the following lawful bases:

- **Contract:** To provide the service you requested
- **Consent:** For analytics and marketing (opt-in)
- **Legitimate Interest:** For security and fraud prevention
- **Legal Obligation:** To comply with applicable laws

### 2.2 Processing Purposes

| Purpose | Lawful Basis | Retention |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Service delivery | Contract | Until account deletion |
| Analytics (opt-in) | Consent | 14 days (configurable) |
| Security | Legitimate interest | 90 days |
| Compliance | Legal obligation | As required by law |

### 2.3 Data Processors

We use the following processors:
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication hosting
- **Vercel:** Application hosting
- **Email service:** Transactional emails (if applicable)

All processors are bound by data processing agreements.

## 3. International Transfers

### 3.1 Transfer Locations

Data may be transferred to:
- United States (Supabase)
- European Union (if applicable)
- Other regions as required for service delivery

### 3.2 Safeguards

- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
- Adequacy decisions where applicable
- Binding Corporate Rules (if applicable)

## 4. Data Retention

### 4.1 Retention Periods

- **Account Data:** Until account deletion or 2 years of inactivity
- **Usage Events:** 14 days (configurable, default)
- **Audit Logs:** 90 days
- **Support Tickets:** 1 year after resolution

### 4.2 Deletion

- Deletion requests processed within 30 days
- Backup data retained for up to 90 days for security
- Anonymized data may be retained for analytics

## 5. Data Subject Rights (DSR)

### 5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of your data:
- Via `/account/export`
- Via API: `/api/privacy/export`
- Via email: privacy@example.com

### 5.2 Right to Rectification

You can correct inaccurate data:
- Update profile in Settings
- Contact support for corrections

### 5.3 Right to Erasure

You can delete your data:
- Via Settings → Privacy → Delete Account
- Via API: `/api/privacy/delete`
- Via email: privacy@example.com

### 5.4 Right to Portability

You can export your data:
- Format: JSON
- Includes: Account data, usage events, preferences
- Excludes: Audit logs (for security)

### 5.5 Right to Restriction

You can limit processing:
- Disable analytics in Settings
- Contact support for other restrictions

### 5.6 Right to Object

You can object to processing:
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- Disable analytics tracking
- Contact support for other objections

### 5.7 Response Times

- Access requests: 30 days
- Deletion requests: 30 days
- Rectification requests: 7 days
- Other requests: 30 days

## 6. Children's Data

### 6.1 Age Restrictions

Our service is not intended for users under 16 (or 13 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

### 6.2 Parental Controls

If you are a parent/guardian:
- Contact us to review or delete your child's data
- We will delete data if we become aware it belongs to a child

## 7. Security

### 7.2 Organizational Measures

- Incident response procedures
